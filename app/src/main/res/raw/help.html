Creating deck of flashcards

Anytime you think that something is not understandable you should tap on the screen of video player. Subtitle is put on marked list which is seen below the player or can be slide from left side.
Item on that list are link to rewind movie to moment of text occurrence

When you click MARKED (right top corner) than you see list of candidates for flashcards. Feel free to make some simple action:

  • delete
  • merge two flashcards
  • edit
